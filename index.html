OUR HISTORY

S & C Pools is a family owned and operated company which started in the early 80’s. We began building inground vinyl liner swimming pools out of our homes and soon after opened two retail locations in King, NC and then Greensboro.

As of March 2021 we have closed the Greensboro retail store and operate solely out of King, NC.

OUR PRESENT

After over thirty years of installing in-ground vinyl liner swimming pools we are no longer constructing new pools. We are dedicating our time and abilities to servicing existing pools. Our services include the retail sales of chemicals, supplies, and equipment as well as all types of repairs to existing pools. We specialize in replacing vinyl liners.
